Oilfield Drilling Jobs provides the BIGGEST job listings for drilling people on the mobile. Up-to-date listings ensure you can get the latest jobs from around the world!


Using the Oilfield Drilling Jobs mobile application ensures that you can be one of the first to find and apply for jobs.


The mobile application will automatically update daily with new jobs. Just browse the job listings and click to read more information. Once synchronised, you can read about the jobs offline, there is no need for an internet connection.


You will be able to receive automated notifications on the application as well as in your email for any jobs that you are interested in. All you need to do is tell the application your desired job positions and or locations, for example "Drilling Supervisor", "Company Man", "North Sea".


If you see some jobs that you like, simply make them your favourites and choose to send the job information to your email. From there, you can easily open the link and apply directly to those jobs. Be quick and get that job first!


Happy job hunting!
